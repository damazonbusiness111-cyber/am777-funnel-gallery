FAQ

Clear expectations.
No hidden confusion.

Use this section to answer common questions before a client asks for a quote.

What if I only need a simple page?

Choose Portfolio / CTA Page. It is for a simple business or portfolio page with direct contact buttons only.

What if I already have a website?

Use the System Gallery or Estimator. Existing website automation may cost more because compatibility needs to be checked.

Is domain included?

Custom domain is not included. Free GitHub Pages publishing can be used for starter setup.

Is the chatbot a real AI agent?

Lower packages include a chatbot-style inquiry snippet. Advanced AI chatbot or AI receptionist is estimated separately.

Is CRM included in Funnel + CTA Setup?

No. CRM starts with Funnel + Google Sheets CRM.

Can you add invoice and follow-up automation?

Yes. Invoice and follow-up workflows are available as advanced add-ons or estimator-based setup.

Can pricing change?

Yes. The estimator gives approximate range only. Final quote depends on volume, business process, website structure, tools, and automation needs.

Is monthly support required?

No. Monthly CRM care, monitoring, and debugging are optional.
